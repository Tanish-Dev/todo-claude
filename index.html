Why Things

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built to be fast and stay out of your head — because the best productivity tool is one you barely notice.

No account, no cloud
Everything stays in your browser. Your tasks never leave your device — no server sees them, ever.
Instant, no load time
Opens in under a second. No spinner, no skeleton screens, no waiting. It's just there when you need it.
Built for real people
No complicated project hierarchies or subtask nesting. A task is a task. Mark it done, move on.
Day & night mode
Glassmorphism UI that looks good in both dark and light — and remembers which you prefer.
Progress at a glance
A quiet progress bar at the top shows how far you've come today. Feels good to watch it fill up.
Mobile-first design
Touch targets sized for thumbs, safe-area insets for notches, and no tap delay. Feels native.
